Title: Type Treatments
----
Text: 

Type treatments for both SK&A and ETI brands use a contemporary, humanist sans-serif called Gibson, along with an (optional) headline font called Century Modern.

For main headlines, either Gibson or Century Mondern can be used, depending on context. Gibson (being sans-serif) is perhaps more readable in very small applications, and may also feel slightly more casual than Century in some contexts.

**Note:** all "em" font sizes given below use a base font size of 16px.

***

# Main headline example

**font:** Century Modern Roman

**print:**
- 48pt / 48pt
- kerning: -25

**screen:**
````html
font-family: 3em/1 "Century-Modern", "Georgia", "Palatino", Times, serif;[
letter-spacing: -0.0375em;
color: #0F1F2D;
````

***

<h1 class="alt">Main headline example (alternate)</h1>

**font:** Gibson Regular

**print:**
- 48pt / 48pt
- kerning: -5

**screen:**
````html
font-family: 3em/1 "Gibson", "Helvetica-Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;
letter-spacing: 0;
color: #0F1F2D;
````
